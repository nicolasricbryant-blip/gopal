Before we start

GoPal needs a few permissions to watch for drowsiness and reach your contacts if something's wrong:

  • Camera — watches your eyes/face on-device. Video never leaves your phone.
  • Microphone — lets the assistant hear your check-in replies.
  • Location — included in alerts so contacts know where you are.

You can decline any of these — GoPal will run with reduced functionality (e.g. no voice check-ins without mic, no location in alerts).
